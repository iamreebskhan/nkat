# Data Processing Addendum (Template)

> **NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** Counsel reviews every instance.

This Data Processing Addendum ("**DPA**") supplements the MSA between
`[Customer]` and `[Company]`. It applies to processing of Personal Data
that is not separately governed by the BAA (Exhibit B).

## 1. Roles

Customer is the Controller (or Business under CPRA). Company is the
Processor (or Service Provider). Where Company determines the means and
purposes of processing for de-identified analytics or product
improvement, it does so as a separate Controller and only on lawful basis.

## 2. Processing Instructions

Company processes Personal Data only on Customer's documented
instructions, including those in the MSA, this DPA, and the Order Form.
Company will inform Customer if it believes an instruction violates
applicable law.

## 3. Categories of Data + Data Subjects

| Category | Data Subjects |
|---|---|
| Account / contact info (names, emails, phone, role) | Customer's workforce |
| App usage logs (per-user) | Customer's workforce |
| Provider-identifiers (NPI, taxonomy) | Providers Customer bills for |
| PHI (under separate BAA) | Patients of Customer's clients |

## 4. Sub-processors

Same list as BAA § 5 plus any non-PHI sub-processors (e.g., HubSpot for
marketing CRM if applicable). Updated sub-processor list at [link];
30-day prior notice for new sub-processors with right to object.

## 5. Security

Same Security Rule + organizational controls as BAA § 3, plus:

- ISO 27001-aligned policies (Vanta).
- SOC 2 Type 2 audit annually.
- Pen test annually + ad-hoc on major architecture change.

## 6. Data-Subject Rights

Company will assist Customer in fulfilling Data-Subject Rights requests
within statutory windows:

| Jurisdiction | Window |
|---|---|
| CPRA (CA) | 45 days, extendable 45 |
| VCDPA / CTDPA / CPA / TDPSA / others | 45 days |
| WMHMDA (WA) | 45 days; consent withdrawal must be honored same day |
| GDPR (if/when EU) | 1 month, extendable 2 |

Right types: access, deletion, correction, portability, opt-out of
profiling/sale/sharing, withdraw consent.

## 7. Cross-Border Transfers

US-only Phase 1 — no cross-border. Template anticipates EU Standard
Contractual Clauses (SCCs) on EU expansion; UK Addendum where applicable.

## 8. Audits

Customer may, on reasonable notice and at most once per 12 months,
request the SOC 2 + pen-test summary; on-site audits available only if
SOC 2 has a finding affecting Customer's data.

## 9. Breach Notification

For non-PHI Personal Data: Company notifies Customer of confirmed
unauthorized disclosure within **72 hours**. PHI-specific timing per BAA
§ 4.

## 10. Return / Deletion

On termination, Personal Data deleted within 30 days unless legal
retention requires longer (e.g., HIPAA audit log § 7.3 of MSA).

## 11. Washington MHMDA Specifics

For Washington-resident Data Subjects:

- Sensitive consumer-health data is processed only with prior, separate,
  freely-given consent.
- Consent withdrawal honored same business day; deletion follows DSR
  process.
- No sale/sharing of consumer-health data.
- Privacy notice published at [link]; appeals process available within
  the same notice.

## 12. Colorado AI Act (SB24-205) Specifics

If the Service is determined to be a "high-risk AI system" under SB24-205
(currently exempt under healthcare-AI carve-out), Company will: (i)
provide impact assessment summaries on request; (ii) provide notice of
material algorithmic change; (iii) cooperate in Customer's own deployer
obligations to consumers.
